The Perfect PB&J

Description

This peanut butter and jelly sandwich is my favorite sandwich. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when made right.

    Ingredients

  • 2 slides of white bread
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 jar of peanut butter
  • A butter knife
  • A cutting board

    Directions

  1. Lay both slides of bread next to each other
  2. Spread peanute butter on the left slice of bread
  3. Spread jelly on the right slice of bread
  4. Carefully put the two halves together
